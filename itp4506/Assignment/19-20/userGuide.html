User Guide

  • groupCustomers Guide
      Search Menu
    1. Input You want to eat in Search Box, if the result is empty the system will display the link to let you see all restaurant
    2. You can see list of the restaurants, and show their food tag as well as mark of the restaurant. In additon, you can utlize the multi selector to display the restaurants.
    3. Click the Restaurant card to display the restaurant menus and details .

      Comment and Rate to Restaurant
    1. Input the restaurant name to searching box
    2. click the restaurant card
    3. you can write the comment in the page bottom

      Add and remove Favorite restaurants
    1. In the restaurant list page, you can click the "Love" icon button to save your favorite restaurants
    2. You can see your favorite restaurants list when click the menu button on top of the page and click the Favorite restaurants button
    3. When you need to remove the favorite restaurant, you can remove in the restaurant list or favorite restaurant list which has the remove icons

      Edit information
    1. Click the Meun button
    2. The navigator bar will display and Click the Personal Information
    3. When edit your inforamtion after, you click the update button
  • restaurantOperator Guide
      Create Restaurants
    1. Login Your Operator Account
    2. When your account upgrade the rule to Operator will redirect to Operator page
    3. Click the "Add restaurant" card will display windows
    4. You need to input your restaurant information
    5. Create Your Menu Catalog such as Breakfast, Dinner...
    6. Add the food as well as the price and select the suitable menu Catalog
    7. Click the create button if the form checking is pass will display the System saved message

      Edit Restaurant
    1. Click the restaurant card more button and click the edit button
    2. Input you want to edit information
    3. Click the Save button the information will saved
      Delete Restaurant
    1. Click the restaurant card more button and click the Delete button

      Edit information
